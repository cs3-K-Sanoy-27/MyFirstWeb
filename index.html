Gutom ko!

By: Khent Sanoy

Pizza pasta carbonara lasagna

mukaon ko if librehon ko

laag ta, gawas ta, mall to mall, cafe to cafe

palit og milktea gikan sa Pretea

palit og french fries bahalag dako ang price

greenwich or dunkin lami ilang everything

mcdo or jollibee chowking or kfc

walay absent basta libre :)
